Skullgirls

Skullgirls is a 2D fighting game released in 2012 developed by Lab Zero Games. It can be played as a team based fighting game similar to the Marvel vs. Capcom series or a solo fighter more in line with Street Fighter. The soundtrack features tracks composed by Michiru Yamane, most known for her work on the Castlevania series of games. Skullgirls recieved generally positive reviews at launch but has garnered more praise as of late due to its implementation of rollback netcode allowing for smoother online matches across greater distances and connection quality. The game originally launched with eight playable characters but has since expanded to fourteen.
