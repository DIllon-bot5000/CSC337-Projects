Epic Poems: Paradise Lost
Dante's Inferno Paradise Lost

Paradise Lost is an epic poem written by John Milton and was first published in 1667. It is considered Milton's greatest work and was controversial upon release as it was intially believed that Satan was the protagonist of the story. The poem intially follows the story of Satan after he is expelled from Heaven after leading a rebellion against God. The war is recounted from various perspective until the story shifts and follows Adam and Eve while they live in the Garden of Eden until they are expelled after breaking the one rule of the garden.
